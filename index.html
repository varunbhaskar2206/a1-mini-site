What is CosPlay? Dressing Up Is Fun....

Cosplay, a portmanteau of the words costume play, is a performance art in which participants called cosplayers wear costumes and fashion accessories to represent a specific character. Cosplayers often interact to create a subculture and a broader use of the term "cosplay" applies to any costumed role-playing in venues apart from the stage. Favorite sources include manga and anime, comic books and cartoons, video games, and live-action films and television series.
